Secure Your Sensitive PDF Documents

Our free online PDF protection tool helps you add passwords and permissions to your PDF files, ensuring only authorized users can access or modify your sensitive documents.

Why Protect Your PDFs?

  • Prevent unauthorized access: Keep confidential documents secure with passwords
  • Control usage rights: Restrict printing, editing or copying of your PDFs
  • Professional security: Add watermarks to identify protected documents
  • No software needed: Works directly in your browser with no installation

How to Use Our PDF Protector

  1. Upload your PDF by dragging and dropping or clicking to browse
  2. Set open password (optional) and owner password (recommended)
  3. Configure document permissions as needed
  4. Add watermark if desired (optional)
  5. Click "Protect PDF" to secure your file
  6. Download the protected PDF

Security Features

Our tool uses strong 128-bit AES encryption to protect your PDF documents. All processing happens in your browser - your files are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy.
